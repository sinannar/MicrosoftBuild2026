## Abstract

Are you a community leader or looking for more ways to get involved with your local developer community? Interested in creating your own community? Join Azure Tech Group (ATG) leaders to learn how ATG connects developers, cloud engineers, and tech enthusiasts through local meetups, networking, and collaboration. Discover how to start and grow a community, connect with Microsoft experts, share real-world AI and Azure experiences, and engage with the broader Azure technical ecosystem.
