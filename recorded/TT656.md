## Abstract

.NET 8 and 9 both hit end of life on November 10, 2026. With hundreds of repos to upgrade, manual migration won't cut it—and pointing agents at the problem without structure burns tokens and produces inconsistent results. Let's talk about how agents, given the right deterministic tools, can move your .NET portfolio from rigid and locked-in to liquid—continuously modern, with swappable components and routine upgrades instead of fire drills. Bring your migration questions and stack horror stories.
